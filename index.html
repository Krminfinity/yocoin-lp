Welcome to YoCoin

The Meme Token of Yokohama City University

What is YoCoin?

YoCoin (YOC) is a blockchain-based meme token created by and for the students of Yokohama City University. It represents fun, community, and the future of student engagement in the Web3 era.

Why YoCoin?

  • Reward campus event participation
  • Vote on university festival content (Enka Festival)
  • Exchange for goods and services in the student ecosystem
  • Connect with fellow students through Web3

How to Get Started

  1. Create a wallet (e.g., MetaMask)
  2. Join our Telegram or Discord
  3. Earn YoCoin through events, missions, and engagement
  4. Use YoCoin to interact with the YCU community
